About Us

Built Around Practical Technology

Celeste IT helps businesses improve operations through smarter systems, better integrations, and technology solutions that work in real business environments.

Who We Are

We focus on technology that makes business easier to run

Celeste IT is built around practical experience in retail operations, digital commerce, system coordination, and business process improvement. We understand that most businesses do not need more complexity — they need systems that work better.

Our role is to help businesses improve the way technology supports day-to-day operations. That means reducing manual work, improving visibility, solving workflow issues, and building a stronger digital and operational foundation.

We work with a practical mindset. We do not believe in overcomplicating systems or delivering solutions that look good only on paper. We focus on what actually helps businesses operate more efficiently and grow with confidence.

What we focus on:

  • Practical technology solutions
  • Stronger business operations
  • Better-connected systems
  • Smarter workflows and automation

Meet the Founder

Janik Jayasuriya

Founder of Celeste Daily

Celeste IT is driven by the vision of Janik Jayasuriya, founder of Celeste Daily and a leader with deep experience in retail and commerce operations.

Having worked closely with the challenges of scaling businesses, managing systems, and improving operational efficiency, he recognized that many technology solutions fail to address real business needs.

Celeste IT was built to change that focusing on practical execution, system alignment, and technology that genuinely supports how businesses operate.

“Technology should support real business operations not complicate them.”

Our Vision

How We Work

What Makes Our Approach Different

We combine operational understanding with practical implementation, so technology decisions lead to real business improvements.

Business-First Thinking

Operational Alignment

Practical Execution

We focus on solutions that are usable, scalable, and relevant to real business needs.

Long-Term Support

We aim to be a dependable partner, not just a team that appears for one setup and disappears.
